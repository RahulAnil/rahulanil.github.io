United Benetton Campaign Ad

This unit in my personal opinion was the best unit this semester. The summative project we had to do was really fun. For our Benetton Ad Campaign we were tasked with choosing an “ism” and portraying it using a picture. Pictures do speak thousands of words and multiple emotions can be displayed in an image. For this project my partner (Varun) and I decided to choose classism. It affects a large group of people and it's an issue that still is very common today. For our campaign, we wanted our posters to have a theme. Certain elements that would make both our posters look related and people could identify as part of the same organization. This lead to our decision to use same characters for both posters and have the iconic tape. For my poster, I decided to have each person from each pay bracket sit together and be on the same level. They are all communicating with each other to show that class doesn't matter.

I used green tape because money is often associated to be green and green really pops out from the rest of the background and in addition it contrasted with the rest of the image. In this project, I also used clones of myself by taking the skills we learned from the previous task we did and I applied it to this one. In the future, I will be using the photoshop skills I learned in this unit and applying them to other subjects such as english when we are required to create a poster and I even plan to use this when I go out and just take pictures for fun. Overall, I was really happy with the end result and felt that I demonstrated some of my best work in this project.
