Frog Prince Assignment

The graphic communication unit was a meticulous yet fun unit. For our summative we were tasked with taking an existing poster from a small non-profit organization and redesign it. Varun and I were tasked with redesigning a poster for an organization known as Scarborough Cycles. Their mission is to make more people bike to work which not only reduces the amount of cars on the road but also keeps people healthy. With this in mind, I chose a very simple yet effective way to design my poster. I decided to keep my poster very minimal and have just the information it needed. My biggest problem with the poster scarborough cycles created was that it was too bland and it was just unappealing to look at.

I made my poster very vivid and choose colours that would contrast each other in order to stand out. I only selected two types of font so that it wouldn't look too inconsistent and made sure both fonts had some sort of unity. In addition for my final version of the poster I used colours from the bike in the image I selected. Also I followed text hierarchy like we learned in the lesson and made the more important information larger and more visible. From this project, I will take away skills such as design layout, text hierarchy, and colour theory. I will apply this in future projects down the road such as my own videos and even when i'm creating posters for other classes.
