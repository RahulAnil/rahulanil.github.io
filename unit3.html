Music Mixing

A small fun mini assignment we did to better understand audacity and how to manipulate audio. We had to incorporate our voices and other sounds into it.

Podcasts

Sound boarding was a very interesting unit. It was something I wasn’t really familiar with as I don’t work with just audio by itself. Our summative was to create a podcast where we talk about anything we wanted to so once again I partnered with Varun to create a podcast about film; one of the topics I am really interested in. In order to make the podcast more interesting we also got our two close friends to talk in the podcast as they too had a liking towards film. When we created the podcast, it was a lot harder than we thought. Keeping conversations going and speaking clear is a lot harder than it may seem. Coming up with insightful things to say and making sure we weren't just rambling on was also tricky. When it came to editing the final audio files in audacity, we had to keep in mind that audio levels should be consistent and that any speech imperfections must be edited out. That was what took the longest when i was editing the podcast. In addition, I added background music, an intro to our fake podcast show, and even a commercial at the end to make the podcast more legitimate. In the end we did manage to make in my opinion a really good podcast. We made sure we included all the elements and things you would hear in an actual podcast and put a lot of effort in this project.
